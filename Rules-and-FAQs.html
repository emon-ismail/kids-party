Rules and FAQs

Read Our Indoor Playground Rules For A Riveting Time!

Me Land oftentimes feels like so much fun that you might think we’ve adopted an “anything goes” policy. That couldn’t be further from the truth. To build a fun, safe, and secure family entertainment center, we’ve set a few rules for everyone to follow. Your attendance constitutes acceptance of these rules and conditions for play, so please read through them carefully

Me Land’s Dos and Don’ts

  • Please stay home if you, or your child, aren’t feeling well.
  • Hand sanitizer will be provided before entering the play area.
  • No adult entry without a child.
  • Waivers are required for ALL guests before entry.
  • Our facility is designed for children ages 12 and under.

While Playing At Me Land

  • Children must be supervised by a parent or caregiver at all times in the play areas.
  • Parents/guardians assume full responsibility and liability for children while on the premises. Do not leave your children unattended.
  • We are not responsible for loss, theft, or damage to your personal property.
  • This is a sock-only facility. Socks must be worn by ALL guests (and are available for purchase at the front).
  • Non-skid socks are required in Tot Zone.
  • Me Land is a nut-free facility.
  • Exit and re-entry are subject to facility capacity.

Me Land’s Dos and Don’ts

  • Slide one person at a time in a forward-seated position, feet first.
  • No climbing up slides.
  • No climbing or standing on the outside of the play equipment.
  • Don’t throw balls outside of the ball pit area.
  • No running.
  • No hitting, biting, kicking, punching, or pushing.
  • Be respectful of other children.
  • Please abide by all age limits of designated items and equipment.
  • No outside food is permitted.
  • No food is allowed inside the play zones.

Please feel free to reach out to us with any questions, concerns, or if you need clarification on the rules before your next visit.

Please note: We charge regular weekend rates on holidays
