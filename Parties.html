Parties

Plan Your Parties At Our Family Entertainment Center

With pre-defined public parties and custom private parties, Me Land offers many different options to bring your next event to life. Take a look through our many selections, then give us a call to perfectly customize your party. Don’t forget to read through our Party Details to make sure you understand all the important information before booking!

Public Parties

Choose between our Monkey, Tiger, and Lion parties, all designed to host a specific number of kids for one inclusive party fee. You’ll get over an hour of indoor playground time alongside 45 minutes of party room use. To add more guests to your party, spend just $15 (or $20 on weekends) for each additional child.

Private Parties

Party privately in style with your own host/hostess, catered pizza, and 1 devoted hour of private playtime fun (plus an hour of private party room time while we open to the public). We’ll even share electronic full-color custom invitations you can download, print, and give out so all attendees know that you’re throwing a Me Land bash! For a $5 service fee, we’ll customize the electronic invitations’ information for you.

    Public Parties

    1 hour (indoor playground) + 45 mins (party room) + 15 min (clean up) requires $150 deposit to book.

    Please note: Public parties happen concurrently with walk-ins. Your group will play first, then get private party room use for the last 45 minutes of your scheduled party slot.

    Private Parties

    1 hour (indoor playground) + 45 mins (party room) + 15 min (clean up) requires $250 deposit to book.

    Please note:We’ll be open to the public during your party room time, but our party room is located in a private, enclosed area.

    Public & Private Party Features

    • Admission for party guests and adult chaperones (2 per child)
    • Party host
    • Basic set up and clean up
    • Electronic invitations
    • Plates, napkins, and utensils
    • Pizza and choice of a juice box or bottled water (for children guests only)
    • Party Extras/Add-Ons (not included): chicken wings, extra pizza, and beverages for adult guests

    All Me Land Party Guidelines

    • Bring your own cake, cutter, and candles. No ice cream cake or confetti
    • Some outside food allowed
    • Socks and signed waiver are required for ALL guests (both participating and non-participating)
    • Holidays are charged at weekend rates

    Cancellation Policy

    Your non-refundable $150 (or $250 for private parties) deposit will be charged when you reserve your event. We require at least 2 weeks' notice (by phone or email) to reschedule your party. Your paid deposit will be applied to any new date chosen within the next 60 days. For any other cancellations or reschedules that fall outside this policy, you’ll forfeit your paid deposit.
