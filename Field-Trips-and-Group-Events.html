Field Trips and Group Events

Host Your Next Event At Maryland’s Top Indoor Playground

Me Land creates a lively, open space, and soft play location for kids of all ages to explore their imagination–one zone at a time. When you let children play, they get to foster creativity while developing creative thinking, physical strength, cognitive abilities, and emotional intelligence, all at the same time. Bring the whole class (or group) to a safe, engaging place to explore and build on those abilities.

Group Package A

Includes 2 hours of play

Group Package B

Includes 2 hours of play + food and drink (for kids only)

Booking your next field trip or group event is easy. To get started, email us with this info:

  • Number of kids
  • Requested date and time
  • Choose Package A or B
  • Tell us about yourself and your group.
